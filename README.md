
# Task 02 – Prompting for Creativity

## Base Prompt
Generate a short story about a student who discovers a hidden talent.

---

## Prompt Variation 1 – Friendly Tone
Write a short, friendly, and motivational story about a college student who discovers a hidden talent. Keep the language simple and inspiring.

### Output
The story focuses on encouragement and positivity, making the character relatable and uplifting.

---

## Prompt Variation 2 – Dramatic Tone
Write a dramatic short story about a college student who discovers a hidden talent during a difficult phase of life. Use emotional language.

### Output
This version is more intense and emotional, with deeper feelings and conflict.

---

## Prompt Variation 3 – Creative & Imaginative
Write a highly creative and imaginative short story about a college student who discovers a magical hidden talent. Add vivid descriptions.

### Output
The output is rich in imagination, detail, and visual elements, making it more engaging.

---

## Comparison & Conclusion
Different prompt structures and tones result in varied outputs.  
Creative prompts with clear tone and style instructions produce more engaging and detailed responses.
